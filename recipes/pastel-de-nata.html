Pastel de nata Português

Description

Portuguese egg tarts, also known as Pastéis de Nata, are a popular and delicious Portuguese pastry.

The pastry consists of a flaky and buttery crust that encases a creamy and rich egg custard filling.

Ingredients

  • A puff of pastry dough
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon powder (for dusting)
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting too)
